How to protect yourself and your families?


Self-protection

Cleaning

  • Clean and disinfect frequently touched items daily
  • Disinfect anything you brought from the outside of your home using 90% alcohol
  • Regular cleaning can also help you stay organized and positive throghout this time
  • Keep in mind that the virus can live up to two weeks. Therefore, sanitize your parcels and packages if needed

Protecting your community

  • It's important to understand that this virus could bring potential risks to people around you, espically elderly and people with underlying conditions
  • Respect others. Practice social distancing religiouly and aviod close contact if you have been exposed to high risk people
  • ALWAYS keep your mask on for your own safety and safety for others/li>
  • Be honest. You should inform anyone with who you had close contact if you are tested positive
  • Help each other. While close contact is discouraged, you can always help out people who are in need, simple gestures such as dropping off groceries, greeting people over the phone. This will help people relieve their mental stress
