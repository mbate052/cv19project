How to protect yourself and your families?


Self-protection

  • Wear a medical mask
  • Keep at least 6 feet apart from others in the public
  • Use hand sanitizer to wash your hand for at least 10 seconds
  • Or you can use normal soap for at least 25 seconds
  • Never touch your mask using your head before washing
  • Wear masks over both your mouth and nose

Cleaning

  • Clean and disinfect frequently touched items daily
  • Disinfect anything you brought from the outside of your home using 90% alcohol
